Life Expectency

Our Project

On the site the team has developed a multiple regression model using data from the World Bank website (click image
below to connect to their website). From the World Bank site we are able to extract numerous factors looking
into how the life expectancy on a certain country can vary.

With COVID-19 barricading our lifes, consuming all the news and social media during 2020, it was only
natural for our curiosity to wander into finding how specific aspects of our life and environment can impact
our long-term well-being. Thusly, our team has put in the work to develop a model in attempt to understand how
well these distinct variables can impact a more expansive area like life expectancy.
