Life Expectency

Our Project

Life expectancy is one of the key metrics to assess population health of any country.

It is affected by multiple factors such as access to healthcare, birth rates, availability of sanitation resources, prevalence of different communicable and non-communicable disease.
In this project, we use multiple linear regression model to predict life expectancy of any country based on crude birth rate, fertility rate and Tuberculosis incidence per 100,000 people.
